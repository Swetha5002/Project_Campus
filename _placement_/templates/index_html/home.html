John Doe

Google

12 LPA

15 May 2023

Success Story

John shares his journey of how he prepared for interviews and cracked Google with his consistent efforts. He emphasizes the importance of data structures and algorithms in the interview process.

"The placement cell at VCET was extremely helpful in guiding me through the preparation process. The mock interviews conducted by the department faculty helped me gain confidence. I started my preparation early in my third year, focusing on problem-solving skills and system design concepts."

John also participated in various coding competitions which helped him improve his problem-solving speed. He recommends practicing on platforms like LeetCode and CodeChef regularly.

Key Takeaways:

  • Start preparation early (3rd year)
  • Focus on DSA fundamentals
  • Participate in coding competitions
  • Utilize college placement resources

Jane Smith

Microsoft

10 LPA

22 June 2023

Success Story

Jane explains how participating in college projects helped her stand out during the Microsoft interview process. She worked on an IoT-based project under faculty guidance which became a talking point in her interviews.

"The project experience I gained at VCET gave me practical knowledge that complemented my theoretical understanding. Microsoft interviewers were particularly interested in my approach to solving real-world problems through engineering."

Jane advises juniors to actively participate in departmental projects and hackathons. She credits her success to the strong foundation in communication systems she received from the ECE department faculty.

Key Takeaways:

  • Get involved in practical projects
  • Develop problem-solving mindset
  • Participate in hackathons
  • Leverage faculty guidance

Robert Johnson

Amazon

15 LPA

10 April 2023

Success Story

Robert talks about his internship experience that eventually led to a full-time offer from Amazon. He completed a summer internship at Amazon after his third year which converted to a PPO.

"The internship was challenging but the problem-solving skills I developed through my coursework at VCET helped me excel. My manager was impressed with my ability to quickly learn new technologies and apply them to solve problems."

Robert emphasizes the importance of soft skills along with technical knowledge. He participated in various extracurricular activities and student chapters which helped him develop communication and leadership skills that were valuable in the corporate environment.

Key Takeaways:

  • Pursue quality internships
  • Develop both technical and soft skills
  • Be adaptable to new technologies
  • Engage in extracurricular activities
